# Post-Launch SEO Verification Plan

Planning only; none of these actions were performed.

1. Confirm DNS resolves to the rebuilt production site.
2. Confirm SSL and one-hop canonical host normalization.
3. Purge SiteGround, WordPress, CDN, and browser caches.
4. Confirm homepage returns HTTP 200.
5. Confirm production robots.txt.
6. Confirm production is indexable.
7. Confirm staging remains noindexed or inaccessible.
8. Confirm sitemap index and all child sitemaps return HTTP 200.
9. Submit the production sitemap to Google Search Console.
10. Test a representative and high-value set of unchanged URLs.
11. Test every approved redirected URL.
12. Test retired URLs for the approved 404/410 response.
13. Confirm redirects resolve in one hop.
14. Confirm production-domain self-referencing canonicals.
15. Confirm WooCommerce product schema.
16. Confirm article schema.
17. Confirm hub indexation and unique search intent.
18. Monitor server/Rank Math 404 reports.
19. Monitor soft 404s in Search Console.
20. Monitor crawl anomalies and sitemap exclusions.
21. Update internal links that still pass through redirects.
22. Review Search Console after 48–72 hours.
23. Review Search Console after 7 days.
24. Review traffic, conversions, and rankings after 30 days.
